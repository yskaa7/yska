Providing the best care and nutrition for happy and healthy chickens.

The Whole Chicken

At Bounty Chicken Farm, our whole chickens are a symbol of quality, freshness, and natural farming. Raised with care on our open, free-range pastures, each chicken is nurtured from hatchling to harvest with one goal in mind: delivering the healthiest and most flavorful poultry to your kitchen.

Free-Range Lifestyle

Our chickens at Bounty Chicken Farm enjoy the freedom to roam in open pastures, basking in natural sunlight and breathing in fresh air.

Proper Housing

Safe and spacious coops for comfortable living. We maintain strict hygiene protocols to ensure the health of our flocks.

Natural Feed

We feed our poultry a balanced, vegetarian diet made of locally sourced grains and natural supplements.

Naturally Grown 45-Day Chickens

Naturally Grown 45-Day Chickens

Our chickens are raised for 45 days without artificial boosters, allowing them to grow naturally. This results in firmer meat, richer flavor, and better texture.

Naturally Developed for Meat Quality

Our 45-day chickens grow at a natural pace, resulting in juicier, firmer, and more flavorful meat perfect for any recipe.

Balanced Growth, Better Chicken

Raised without pressure to bulk up fast, our chickens grow strong and healthy, producing clean, tender meat that is visibly clean and naturally golden when cooked.
