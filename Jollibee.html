Jollibee and Bounty Fresh: A Partnership Built on Quality and Trust

At Bounty Chicken Fresh Farm, we believe that great food starts with great farming. Our journey began with a simple goal: to raise healthy, happy chickens in a clean, natural environment and it wasn’t long before that dedication caught the attention of one of the Philippines’ most beloved brands: Jollibee. Jollibee was looking for a partner who could meet their high standards for quality, consistency, and food safety. They didn’t just want any supplier they wanted one that shared their values. After visiting our farm, seeing our operations, and tasting the difference in our chicken, they knew they’d found what they were looking for.
