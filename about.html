About Us

The story of Bounty Fresh Farm starts in the early 1990s. What began as a small family-owned poultry farm in a rural area was driven by the desire to offer more than just ordinary eggs and chicken.

Our Mission

At Bounty Fresh Farm, our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality, and ethically raised poultry products, including eggs and chicken, to families and communities. We are dedicated to producing farm-fresh eggs and chicken with the highest standards of animal welfare, ensuring that our products are free from antibiotics, hormones, and harmful chemicals. Our commitment extends beyond just providing food; it is about enriching lives by providing nutritious, delicious, and responsibly produced products that customers can trust and enjoy.

We believe in being stewards of the land and its resources, and we are committed to sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and promote biodiversity. Our mission also includes a commitment to our local community, contributing to the economy by creating jobs, supporting local businesses, and educating future generations about the importance of ethical farming practices.

Our Vision

At Bounty Fresh Farm, our vision is to be the leading provider of ethically produced poultry products—specifically fresh eggs and chicken—setting the standard for the industry in quality, animal welfare, and sustainability. We aim to be known for our dedication to the health of our animals, the health of our planet, and the well-being of the people who consume our products. We envision a future where consumers across the country can enjoy farm-fresh eggs and high-quality chicken without concerns about hormones, antibiotics, or harmful practices.

We seek to revolutionize the poultry industry by showcasing the value of sustainable and humane farming, demonstrating that producing food ethically can also produce great-tasting, nutritious products. Our vision is to create a ripple effect that influences other farms and businesses to adopt similar practices, ensuring that the future of farming is both healthy and sustainable.

Core Values

At Bounty Fresh Farm, we operate on a foundation built upon several core values that guide every decision we make and every action we take. These values are what make us who we are—compassionate stewards of the land, ethical caretakers of our animals, and passionate providers of fresh, nutritious food. Our core values help us stay true to our mission, vision, and the people and communities we serve.
