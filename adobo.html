Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 2–3 pcs dried bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 11 tbsp brown sugar
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
