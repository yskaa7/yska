Blog 1: Why 45-Day Chickens Are the Healthiest Choice

At Bounty Chicken Farm, we believe that good food starts with responsible farming. That’s why we proudly raise 45-day chickens a practice that sets us apart in a world where speed often takes priority over quality.

Unlike farms that rely on artificial growth boosters to make chickens grow in 30 days or less, we allow our chickens to grow at their natural pace. This not only gives the chickens time to develop strong bones and muscles, but it also results in better-tasting, healthier meat for your family.

Our chickens are never injected with hormones or chemicals. They are raised in a clean, well-ventilated environment and fed with a nutritious, natural diet. This method ensures the final product is not only tender and flavorful but also safe and full of nutrients.

We take pride in doing things the right way—even if it takes more time. Because for us, farming isn't about shortcuts. It’s about earning your trust with every chicken we raise. When you choose Bounty Chicken, you choose a meal that was made with care, time, and integrity.
