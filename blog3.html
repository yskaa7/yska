Blog 3: Farm-to-Table: The Bounty Chicken Way

In today’s fast-paced world, it’s hard to know where your food really comes from. At Bounty Chicken Farm, we make that question easy to answer. We are proud to follow the farm-to-table philosophy, meaning every chicken you enjoy came directly from our farm—no middlemen, no guessing.

From hatching to harvesting, we oversee every step of the process. We raise the chicks ourselves, feed and care for them daily, and ensure they grow up in clean, comfortable surroundings. Once they reach 45 days, we process them in certified, sanitary facilities that follow strict food safety standards.

This full control over the process ensures that you’re getting the freshest, safest, and most flavorful chicken possible. Unlike store-bought frozen meats that sit in freezers for weeks, our chickens go straight from the farm to your kitchen in the shortest time possible.

With Bounty Chicken, you're not just getting meat—you’re getting a product you can trace, trust, and feel proud to serve your family. That’s the real meaning of farm-to-table, and it’s a promise we live by every day.
