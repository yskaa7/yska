Blog 4: Caring for Chickens the Bounty Way

At Bounty Chicken Farm, we believe that how you treat animals shows up in the quality of your food. That’s why we’re dedicated to giving our chickens not just a place to live but a place to thrive.

Our chickens are raised in spacious, clean coops where they can move freely and grow naturally. We use only high-quality, hormone-free feed, and we never overcrowd our chickens. Each batch is closely monitored by trained staff who know how to spot signs of stress or sickness.

We also invest in biosecurity and cleanliness, ensuring our farm meets high standards of hygiene and safety. Proper care doesn’t just benefit the chickens it also results in healthier, tastier meat for our customers.

But beyond systems and schedules, what truly sets us apart is the love and responsibility we put into every step. Farming isn’t just a job for us it’s a way of life. We know that by taking care of our chickens, we’re also taking care of the people who will eventually enjoy them.

That’s the Bounty Chicken way: raising chickens with kindness, quality, and a whole lot of heart.
