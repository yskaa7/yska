Chowking and Bounty Fresh: A Partnership in Flavor and Consistency

Chowking, one of the Philippines’ most beloved Chinese fast-food chains, has always been committed to serving dishes that meet the highest standards of quality and freshness. As the brand expanded and its menu evolved, Chowking recognized the need for a reliable chicken supplier that could consistently deliver safe, tender, and flavorful meat. After a careful search, they chose Bounty Fresh Chicken a name trusted by households and businesses nationwide for its farm-to-fork commitment and strict quality control.
