Frequently Asked Question

Find clear answers to common inquiries about Bounty Fresh Chicken products and standards.

1. What is Bounty Fresh Chicken?

Bounty Fresh Chicken is a trusted brand offering fresh, high-quality, and safe poultry products. Our chickens are grown in carefully monitored environments and processed using world-class systems to ensure freshness from farm to table.

2. Where does Bounty Fresh Chicken come from?

Bounty Fresh Chicken comes from our company-owned and contract farms, where we strictly follow biosecurity measures, animal welfare practices, and the highest standards in poultry farming.

3. How are Bounty Fresh chickens raised?

Our chickens are raised in clean, climate-controlled farms. They are fed with high-quality, hormone-free feeds and are regularly monitored by trained professionals to ensure their health and growth.

4. Are Bounty Fresh chickens safe to eat?

Yes! All Bounty Fresh Chicken products go through strict quality control, and the meat is 100% safe, clean, and fresh. We adhere to local and international food safety standards, including HACCP and ISO certifications.

5. Does Bounty Fresh use hormones or growth enhancers?

No. Bounty Fresh chickens are raised naturally without the use of hormones or artificial growth enhancers. Our chickens reach the ideal market weight through proper care, nutrition, and farm management.

6. How do I store Bounty Fresh Chicken properly?

Fresh chicken should be kept refrigerated at 0–4°C and cooked within 1–2 days. For longer storage, freezing is recommended at -18°C. Always check packaging instructions for best storage practices.
