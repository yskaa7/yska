KFC and Bounty Fresh: Delivering Quality and Taste in Every Bite

Bounty Fresh stood out because of its modern and tightly controlled farm-to-fork process. The chickens are raised in clean, bio-secure environments and are harvested at just the right age for perfect tenderness making them ideal for KFC’s Original Recipe, Hot & Crispy, and other menu favorites. This partnership helps KFC deliver the juicy, flavorful chicken that customers expect every time they order. Beyond product quality, KFC also values Bounty Fresh’s dedication to food safety, traceability, and ethical farming practices. With Bounty Fresh as a supplier, KFC can confidently serve meals that are not only delicious but also responsibly sourced strengthening both brands’ promise of quality and customer satisfaction in every bucket.
