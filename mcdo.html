McDonald's and Bounty Fresh: A Strategic Partnership in Poultry Excellence

McDonald’s selects Bounty Fresh as one of its poultry suppliers due to the company’s unwavering commitment to quality, food safety, and operational excellence. As one of the most reputable and vertically integrated poultry producers in the Philippines, Bounty Fresh manages every stage of the production process from breeding and hatching to growing, processing, and distribution. This comprehensive control ensures the consistent delivery of fresh, high-quality chicken products that meet the stringent standards of global food service operations.
